e - En-Ru - English layout to russian. E.g.: rjhpbyf -> корзина
r - Ru-En - Russian (or ukr/bel/ger) layout to english. E.g.: ифылуе -> basket
d - Default - English letter to russian and russian (or ukr/bel/ger) to english. E.g.: rjhpbyf_ифылуе -> корзина_basket
u - Uppercase. E.g.: oneTWOthree - > ONETWOTHREE
l - Lowercase. E.g.: oneTWOthree - > onetwothree
i - Invert - Uppercase to lowercase and lowercase to uppercase. E.g.: ONEtwoTHREE -> oneTWOthree
